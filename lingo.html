Doggo Lingo

Doggo Lingo is an Internet language trend inspired by a shared love of dogs. Before its invention, when a dog or puppy was being extremely cute or funny, there weren't enough words to describe it. Thus, the Internet created words like doggo, pupper, floof, sploot, boof, bork and much more. These cutesy words appeared in memes and posts and soon the language expanded to include meme phrases that described dog memes.

Words and Phrases

Word Meaning/Function
Doggo/doge/woofer Dog
Pupper/pupperino Puppy
Floof/fluffer A very fluffy doggo/pupper
Cloud A doggo/pupper with a fluffy white coat, much like a cloud
Corgo Corgi
Shibe Shiba
Bork A bark
Boof A huffy sound that's not a bark and not a sneeze
Mlem A sound to describe a dog licking its nose
Sploot When a dog lays down on its belly and hind legs spread wide behind
Doing me a frighten A phrase used to caption pictures of startled dogs
